Silly Platformer - BRIBE THE CHEESE POLICE


A very short game in which you play as Cheese Murderer, a piece of cheese who is a murderer on the run from the police. He must collect 12 coins placed around the pleasant gradient platforms in order to pay the bail and successfully bribe the police off his trail.

Controls:

A - Move left

D - Move right

Space - Jump


Play the game here: https://christinelebxsci.github.io/Silly-Platformer/
